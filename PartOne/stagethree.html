Part II: Focussing Excercise

I would like to you close your eyes if you feel comfortable doing so, or focus on the picture on the screen. Take a moment to think about a place where you have felt comfortable. This can be anywhere that means something to you. You may be visualising some woods you like to walk through, a beach where you once went on holiday or a park you enjoy walking around. Whenever you feel the need to relax try to visualise this place in as much detail as you can. Use your senses to make it as real as possible. Visualise yourself enjoying this place

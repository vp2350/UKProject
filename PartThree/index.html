Programme Advance

Part III: Recap

Before we make a start on today’s session, let’s just quickly remind ourselves of what we did last week.

The ADVANCE Programme aims to help you achieve your goals one step at a time by supporting you to manage your thoughts, feelings and behaviours in different situations. Last week we discussed that when there is conflict in our relationships, we could take a time out, use our ‘just-in-case’ box, and relax ourselves to improve our self-management which will reduce the chances of us hurting our partners.
